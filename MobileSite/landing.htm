My name is Lohue. I am a Graphic Designer, Illustrator & Digital Painter who specialises in creating low fidelity, psychedelia.

This website is the first website I have ever created.
Im sure this is obvious to many.
This is the mobile version of the website & is only acting as a placeholder until I can completely remake the website. I apologise for the lack of presentation but I hope it can serve its purpose as a mobile portfolio :)

The purpose of this website is to act as my professional portfolio as well as an interactive view of my career as an artist. If I ever manage to learn more about website development, I would like to add a store-front but as of right now, this is outside of my skillset.

If you are interested in learning more about me & my ideas, interests & artwork, follow the About tab.

To find any of my socials & places where I post my art follow the Links tab.

To find a compilation of all my current artwork, updated only a day after Patreon follow the Gallery tab.
